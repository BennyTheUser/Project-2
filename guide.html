Water Conservation Guide

Now that you have heard why we need to conserve water, you will need to know ways to conserve water if you want to contribute to the efforts of water conservation. It is important that you follow those tips because wasting water will damage the ecosystems and put already endangered animals at risk. Here are some ways to conserve water.

Kitchen

  • Don’t leave the water running when washing dishes by hand
  • Keep the soap usage at a minimum
  • Don’t defrost foods using water
  • Keep drinking water in the fridge

Bathroom

  • Turn off the water when brushing your teeth
  • Do not use the toilet as a wastebasket or ashtray
  • Minimize water usage in showers

Laundry

  • Wash full loads when washing laundry
  • Use wool dryer balls to speed up drying time of the laundry.

Yard

  • Water plants in the early morning
  • Don’t use a hose for cleaning driveways and sidewalks
  • Don’t water plants on windy or rainy days
